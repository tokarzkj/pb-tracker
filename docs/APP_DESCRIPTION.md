# PB Tracker - Application Description

PB Tracker is a specialized application for paintball players to manage their equipment and track their performance on the field.

## Core Use Cases

### 1. Marker Maintenance Logging
Users can track the health and upkeep of their paintball markers to ensure peak performance and longevity.
- **Maintenance Tasks:** Log specific actions like cleaning, greasing the bolt, replacing screws, or swapping grips.
- **Usage Tracking:** Record the number of paintballs shot since the last maintenance session.
- **Chronology:** Track the date of each maintenance event.

### 2. Outing & Performance Logging
Users can log their sessions at the field to monitor their performance and paint performance.
- **Location:** Record the field or venue played at.
- **Paint Tracking:** Log the brand and grade of paint used.
- **Performance Metrics:** Track eliminations (kills), times eliminated (deaths), and total paintballs shot.
- **Date:** Record the date of the outing.
